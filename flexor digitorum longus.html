flexor digitorum longus

little blurb #addDetail

Origin
Th medial portion of the posterior tibia. Just inferior to the soleal line of the the tibia.

Body
The muscle runs down through the posterior compartment of the leg, and passes posterior to the medial malleolus and deep to the flexor retinaculum. It then passes into the plantar compartment of the foot where it splits into four separate tendons.

Insertion
The base of the distal phalanges of the lateral four digits of the foot.

Innervation
Tibial nerve (S2-S3)

Action
Primarily flexes the lateral four digits of the foot at both interphalangeal joints and the metatarsophalangeal joint. Aids in plantarflexion of the ankle.
